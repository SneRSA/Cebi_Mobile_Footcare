Meet the Heart Behind the Care

Cebi's Mobile Foot Care was founded by Cebisa Diniwe, a passionate wellness professional based in Gqeberha, Eastern Cape. With a deep love for helping others and a strong background in foot and hand care, she created a service that’s both accessible and empowering.

Cebi’s mobile approach means that you don’t have to leave the comfort of your home to receive gentle, hygienic, and professional care. Whether you’re managing chronic foot issues, looking for aesthetic nail services, or just need a bit of TLC, Cebi brings care to your doorstep.

Our mission is simple: Comfort, Confidence, and Care — wherever you are.

We proudly serve clients of all ages — from busy professionals and athletes to seniors and stay-at-home parents. Our treatments are adapted to suit your individual needs and lifestyle.

With the recent expansion into nail care services, clients can now enjoy a full wellness experience in one visit — including gel nails, hand therapy, and more.

Our Mission & Values

Holistic Wellness

We care for your entire wellbeing — not just your feet or hands. Our approach is rooted in relaxation, hygiene, and healing touch.

Compassion & Dignity

Every client is treated with respect and kindness. We create a warm, judgment-free space with every visit.

Convenience & Care

Mobile care means less stress for you. We work around your needs to make self-care easier and more accessible.

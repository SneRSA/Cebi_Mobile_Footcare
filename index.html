Overview - About the Business

Cebi’s Mobile Footcare was founded by Cebisa Diniwe in the Eastern Cape (PE). Since its inception in 2021 and formal registration in September 2022, we have aimed to improve the livelihoods of those suffering from foot conditions and disorders. Our focus is on providing professional foot healthcare services and products, utilizing traditional healing hand massages to offer efficient treatment plans.
